How AcciDetect Works ?

Upload Video

Simply upload your video footage to AcciDetect's platform, where our advanced algorithms will start the analysis.

Detect Accidents

AcciDetect's AI algorithms will carefully analyze the video and promptly determine whether an accident is occurring within the footage..

Timestamp Provided

If an accident is detected, AcciDetect will provide a precise timestamp indicating when the accident occurred in the video, allowing for swift response and action.

Industries We Serve

Insurance industry AcciDetect can help insurance companies identify potentially fraudulent claims. By analyzing video footage, it can determine whether reported accidents actually occurred, helping to prevent false or exaggerated claims.
